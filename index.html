About Me

I am a Data Scientist with a unique journey that has shaped my identity. Earlier in my career, I spent over a decade in the arts and entertainment industry, specializing in technical design and entertainment business. During this time, I successfully met the complex needs of diverse clients, and I had the privilege to work on projects involving renowned afrobeat artists, including Davido, Wizkid, 2-baba, P-Square, and many more.

However, my passion for solving puzzles from a young age, interest in tackling complex problems, and enthusiasm for the role of AI in the future, has fueled my interest and guided my steps toward the field of data science. Now, I'm all about technology. And my passion lies in seeking opportunities to learn, collaborate, add value, and make a notable impact.

My journey is a blend of creativity and technology. I am excited about the endless possibilities that data science offers and its crucial role in shaping the future.
