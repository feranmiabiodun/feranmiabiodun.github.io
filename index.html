About Me

I am a Data Scientist with a unique journey that has shaped my identity. Earlier in my career, I ventured into the world of arts and entertainment through technical design and entertainment business for over a decade, ensuring complex clients' needs are met. During this time, I had the privilege to work on projects invloving renowned afrobeat artists, including Davido, Wizkid, 2-baba, P-Square, and many more.

Now, I'm all about technology. My passion for solving puzzles from a young age, interest in tackling complex problems, coupled with my enthusiasm for the role of AI in the future, has fueled my interest and guided my steps toward the field of data science. My passion lies in seeking opportunities to learn, collaborate, add value, and make a notable impact.

My journey is a blend of creativity and technology. I am excited about the endless possibilities that data science offers and its crucial role in shaping the future.
